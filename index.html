Experience the Summer of a Lifetime with Oxford Summer Courses

Our summer courses offer students the opportunity to study their chosen subject with expert tutors, while also participating in a range of educational and social activities. Our summer courses are located in some of the most historic and beautiful locations in the UK, including Oxford, Cambridge, and London.
From exploring the city's rich history and vibrant culture, to dining at historic college halls and attending formal events, our students are sure to have a unique and unforgettable summer experience. At Oxford Summer Courses, we believe that a summer school experience should be more than just academics, and we strive to provide our students with an enriching and rewarding experience that will help them develop both personally and academically.
