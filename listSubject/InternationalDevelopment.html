International Development Summer School

Engage in the most significant issues of our time by exploring aspects of the developing world and emerging economies

What countries are responsible for driving change in the global economy? How is migration affecting sustainability? What about climate change? Answer pressing questions with confidence and build a solid knowledge by studying International Development with Oxford Summer Courses.
Learn from leading Oxford tutors, mirroring the iconic Oxford teaching style, about how emerging economics are promoting economic, social and political development and adopt new approaches and strategies to tackling developmental issues around the world.
You’ll leave us with the skill set and desire to address real-world problems relating to economic growth, politics, humanitarianism, poverty and the environment. We’ll miss you but we know your work in the wider context is going to make an epic change to so many people’s lives.

Gain the skills and experience you need to excel with a unique, unparalleled learning experience.

Each week, you’ll be able to collaborate with leading academics mirroring the teaching method of the most respected educational establishments in the UK.
As current and past alumni of top universities in the world they’re highly specialised and passionate about their subject you’ll develop your academic career through interactive learning, independent study, seminars, and tutorials, depending on your age group.
